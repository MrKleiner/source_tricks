How to place 1 unit thick brush right in the middle of the unit

So, lets imagine you have to put a glass right in the center of that window.

Side view

Step 1
Create a 2 unit thick brush and place it right in the center of 2 units

Step 2
Press “ctrl + m” and youll see a popup window.
Then press “S” and hit “X, Y, or Z” to choose a required scale axis: “X, Y, or Z”, in my case - its Y.

Now, type 0.5 (or just .5), which means we’re going to scale our brush on Y axis by 50% relative to the brush center.

You can manipulate any transformation method this way:
 ”ctrl + m” --> “R” = Rotate
”ctrl + m” --> “S” = Scale
”ctrl + m” --> “M” = Move
